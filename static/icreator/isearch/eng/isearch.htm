    6. Competitive advantages. What does your invention excel its analogues in?
      1. Conceptual and technical advantages.

        2. For the time being, software when searching information takes into account the coincidence of symbols in the user's inquiry and in a piece of information; the logic words "no", "either", "only" is not taken into account. The meaning of information is not taken into account at all. For example, there is a piece of information "The firm P&Co sells furniture and buys wood". The user makes an inquiry about who "selling wood". The standard search engine will establish coincidence of words "sell" + "wood" and will produce information about the firm P&Co which "sells furniture and buys wood". But the information is wrong. On the other hand sometimes there is no coincidence of letters but the meaning of pieces of information does coincide. The standard search system will not produce information. For example, there is a piece of information "we sell a cottage in the country". The user makes an inquiry about "a private house on sale". The words "cottage" and "house" do not coincide, so the standard system will overlook the piece of information, though it is just what one needs. The proposed System will give "we sell a cottage in the country" using the coincidence of meanings.
